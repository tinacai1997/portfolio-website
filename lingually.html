Lingually

Your immersive language learning application

PROBLEM

Language learners are having a hard time consistently practicing new languages.

I noticed friends and family having a hard time learning new languages. They are initially motivated to learn at first, but slowly taper off over time.
This made me wonder: how might we empower users to learn new languages and create a habit of doing so?
Role: UX/UI Designer (UX Bootcamp Solo Project)
Tools: Adobe XD, Marvel, Google Sheets
Timeframe: May 2021 - Jun 2021

USER INTERVIEWS

Lingually transitioned from a vocabulary to a language app

I interviewed three vocabulary learners to understand how they approached learning and the educational tools they preferred. I organized my observations into a Doing-Thinking-Feeling chart.
I noticed that participants used language apps over strictly vocab apps and focused on their experience with learning new vocab in non-native languages as I interviewed.

KEY RESEARCH INSIGHTS

Language learners preferred practicing through realistic conversations

The doing/thinking/feeling data also showed that users that began learning languages for personal reasons were more motivated to continue doing so. Additionally, interviewees preferred learning in-person and had varying study habits.

PROTO-PERSONA

Designing toward a specific user in mind

I created a persona to focus on an individual that represented my user group. It was helpful to reflect on how my solutions would help my persona during the design process.

USER STORIES

Pinpointing 3 specific problems to solve.

I wrote user stories to cater my solution toward persona-specific problems. This put myself in my persona's perspective and helped narrow down my design scope.

IDEATION + DESIGN

Sketching the preliminary app functions

I created low fidelity wireframes to solve each user story. The low fidelity framework helped me focus on functionality rather than content and UI.

TESTING + ITERATION

Making three major improvements to the design

I tested whether language learners could complete persona-specific scenarios using the app prototype. I organized the data into a usability test report then iterated on the lofi wireframes in order of error severity.
