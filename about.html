My Story 📔

My name is Tina Cai and I'm a Seattle based UX designer & developer looking to grow in the design world. 🌍

I'm an ex-electrical engineer who realized my interests lied in engineering user experiences, not electrical systems.

As a woman having worked in a male-dominant field, I learned we should always be inclusive of anybody and everybody. I design to improve lives and experiences through empathy, perspective, and retrospect.

I enjoy working with a project from conception to development. In my spare time, I dabble in writing front-end development languages. I aim to explore the field further and form a hybrid role of UX designer with developer experience.
