About Me

My Story

Hi! I'm Tina - a UX designer looking to explore and further my career in the design world.

I was born in San Francisco but grew up in Seattle and haven't left since. Growing up as a woman of color, I learned that we should always be inclusive of others regardless of field.

I studied at the University of Washington and graduated with a BS in Electrical Engineering. It took my entire degree, two internships, and a full time job before I realized that electrical engineering wasn't for me. My work revolved around the building engineering industry, and though I enjoyed working with my teammates and clients, I was never passionate about the actual electrical work. I later found that the solutions I designed didn't have to be electrical, but could be put toward improving user experiences instead.

During my free time, I dabble in writing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I plan on exploring these fields further so I can better work with developers and create a hybrid role of UX designer with frontend experience.

As an avid tech user and gamer, I hope to combine my work and interests by bringing my designs to the entertainment industry. I feel the frustration and lack of immersion when something just doesn't quite add up in the middle of a video game. My goal is to make our entertainment technology just that- entertaining. When I'm not designing or gaming, you might likely find me cooking. There's something about creating food from scratch that makes eating and sharing it feel so much more rewarding.
